Easy Taco Skillet

Ingredients

Easy taco skillet bake that uses only one pan. This recipe can be made for under $10!
  • 1 pound ground beef

  • 1 onion, chopped

  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes

  • 2 cups water

  • 1 cup converted rice

  • 1 (1 ounce) package taco seasoning

  • 1 cup shredded Mexican cheese blend

  • 1 cup shredded lettuce

Directions

Heat a large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir beef and onion until beef is browned, about 5 minutes.
Drain. Stir tomatoes, water, rice, and taco seasoning into beef mixture and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until rice is tender, about 25 minutes. Top with Mexican cheese blend and lettuce before serving.
